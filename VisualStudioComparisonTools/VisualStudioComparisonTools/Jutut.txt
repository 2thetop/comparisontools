To use WinMerge as the Compare/Diff tool:

    * Click the Add... button
    * For Extension, type *
    * For Operation, select Compare
    * For Command, browse for C:\Program Files\WinMerge\WinMerge.exe
    * For Arguments, type /x /e /ub /wl /dl %6 /dr %7 %1 %2
    * Click OK to accept
